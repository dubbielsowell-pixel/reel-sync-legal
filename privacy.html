This Privacy Policy explains how ReelSyncApp (“we,” “our,” or “the Service”) collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our application, website, or related services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to operate the Service, including:

  • Videos and media you upload for publishing

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Upload and publish content to platforms you authorize

  • Improve reliability and user experience

3. Third‑Party Platforms

When you connect accounts such as TikTok, Instagram, YouTube, or Facebook, you grant ReelSyncApp permission to upload and publish content on your behalf using their official APIs. We do not store your login credentials.

4. Data Storage & Security

We use industry‑standard security practices to protect your data. Access tokens are encrypted and used only for posting content you authorize.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties. Your information is only transmitted to platforms you explicitly choose to publish to.

6. Your Rights

You may revoke access to any connected platform at any time through that platform’s settings. You may also request deletion of your data by contacting us.

7. Children’s Privacy

ReelSyncApp is not intended for children under 13 or the minimum age required by your region for social media use.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the Service indicates acceptance of any changes.
